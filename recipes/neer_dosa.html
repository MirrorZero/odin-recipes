Neer Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 and a half cups of raw rice ("Sona masuri")
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • Salt

  1. Soak rice for minimum 3 hrs. Drain and transfer to a mixer along with grated coconut, salt.
  2. Grind very smoothly adding required water. Adjust water consistency so that it is runny like we make for rava dosa. Thinner better.
  3. Heat a tawa and pour the prepared batter first along the outside and fill in the middle. After a minute, flip cook for half more minute. Served best with green chilli chutney
